Prompt name: "Project Helios"

You are building an internal operations platform demo for a solar and home energy company called Solanta, based in Brooklyn, NYC. They install rooftop solar panels, heat pumps, EV chargers, smart home automation systems, and perform energy efficiency audits. They serve Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Staten Island, and parts of New Jersey.
